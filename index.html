Philipp Markert

Mobile Security Group · Ruhr University Bochum · philipp.markert@rub.de

I'm a Ph.D. student at the Ruhr University Bochum in Germany and a member of the Mobile Security Group; my advisor is Prof. Dr. Markus Dürmuth. My research covers computer security and human-computer interaction (HCI) with a focus on usable security and authentication.
